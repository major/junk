How to Work Upstream with OpenStack
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

**Abstract:**

Presentation on what it takes tosuccessfully contribute upstream to OpenStack. PTLs and committers from telecom operators, vendors, and OpenStack core developers in the OPNFV Doctor project will discuss key lessons and takeaways from their successful and ongoing contributions to OpenStack. Attendees will also hear from OpenStack PTLsÂ working directly with OPNFV Doctor to incorporate components into OpenStack. Hear details on what works, what doesn't and gain valuable insights on how best to integrate upstream.
